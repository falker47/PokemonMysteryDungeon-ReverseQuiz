Questions
The player will be asked 8 questions, with each question from one of 14 different categories; a category will not be chosen more than once. There is one exception to these rules: if "Fight" is chosen for the "alien invasion" question, a follow-up question is asked that does not count towards the total number of questions. After these questions, the player will be asked for their gender.
